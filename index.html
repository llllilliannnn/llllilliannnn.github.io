law in the cinema

During this Spring festival,a great number of films are on show. Whilst, I notice a unsightly behavior which makes some people uncomfortable——using phones to take a picture of the film screen.
I think this demeanor at least is a kind of infringement. It first violates the right of other audiences to watch the film without interference. Why should we be disturbed by this unethical behavior when we all spend the same money to have fun watching movies.
At the same time,it infringes the copyright of the movie. I think this kind of screen photography is usually published on social media, otherwise it will be meaningless for people who take pictures on the screen. It means that he is using an abnormal way to reproduce part of the content of a work, and its quality is definitely greatly reduced. For creators or publishers, their works are distorted to a certain extent. This is a loss to the original author. In view of the fact that copyright should be a right initiated only by the infringed party, screen photography may be the lightest of many infringements (screen photography is not really different from stealing photography), and copyright owners may not be investigated, but those who do such acts should not legitimize screen photography in the name of “sharing” or “helping propaganda”.
I also searched the Internet,in Hong Kong,there is a fine of about 50000 RMB and imprisonment for 3 months. I am looking forward that Chinese government will make stricter laws like this in the future.

Labor Market

Yesterday in the economic lessons,I leaned about the knowledge of “Labor Market” which is closely related to our real life.

There are many people finding works,but the occupations are scarce. As a result,there are also a wide range of factors that may influence a person’s choice of occupations including wage factors,non-wage factors and other limiting factors.

Wage factors,of course,is the most important element. Generally speaking,the higher the salaries on offer,the more a person would want to do the job. Wage rate means the basic wage a worker receives per unit. I think if I need to find a job,I may think of it first. Due to the life quantity depends on incomes a lot. A high bonuses or commission may attract someone too,but not every job has extra bonuses.

Moreover,some other non-wage factors may be attached importance to by people. I will list a few the most principal reasons. First,some jobs can provide a high degree of job satisfaction,which would result in a higher degree of motivation. For example,most teachers have happiness during their whole teaching career. Not only the success of their students in study,but also receiving thanks from parents. That is the pride of all their lifetime. Second,the length of holidays makes a big difference. Although the government have made laws to set down a minimum length for full-time workers. In Western countries,some companies may provide more holidays for delivery mothers,newly married couples and so on.

From this lesson,I learned that everyone have their different choices for a job. All in all,the most suitable one is the best!

2020-11-28

summary of writing skills

In this whole week,I had lessons on how to do a summary writing excellently in ESL exams. Here are several tips I have learned.

First,we should pay attention to the instruction information to find out the purpose. Then,highlight the keywords in the text. There are usually over 8 points in all. In addition,organizing them well is important and we do not have to use them in order. Next,the most important tip is not only trying to use our own words with same meanings as much as possible,but also using linking verbs and phrases for example thank to(to explain why something happened),despite this(to contrast ideas),before this (to say when something happened) and so on. Finally,our summary must be within the word limit which is around 100 or 120 words.

Although this learning process is a little boring,I truly got this skill in the end. Just as the saying goes:”Nothing is difficult to the man who will try”.All in all,come on！
