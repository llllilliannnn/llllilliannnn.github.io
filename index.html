8.1

These days, I am going deep into a topic which is called Trolley Problem. It is a very famous philosophical issue throughout the history. In the first hypothesis,the car breaks don’t work, five workers working on the track, one worker on another side track. If the trolley car keeps going, the five workers will die. However, most people think the driver should drive to the side track to skill one but sparing five people. Dose it can reduce the damage as more as possible to the public? Economically, if more workers lose their lives in the car accident,the government or the enterprise would have to pay a large amount of compensation to their family. By contrast, someone may say less people can lower the pressure on society’s resources per capital. Both two ideas are right based morality. To sum up, people gesture toward reasons having to do with the intrinsic quality of the act itself, consequences be what they may.

Let artists possess freedom !

The restrictions imposed by the government are growing increasingly strict according to public opinion. I therefore argue that all the unnecessary regulations in art industry should be abolished.

First and foremost, freedom serves as the most indispensable factor in facilitating the creation of artists. It offers more sufficient space for such creator, stimulating more inspiration and imagination during the creation and thus enabling them to thoroughly convey their inner critics about social reality without any concerns. As a result, more unique and impressive art works would arise, which contributes to the fame and fortune of the authors. Apparently, without adequate liberty, the artists could hardly achieve their career success.

On top of this, government can also benefit significantly from allowing the artists to create without limitation. Via abolishing those unnecessary regulations in art industry, a considerable amount of administrative expense can be reduced effectively, saving the finite fiscal budgets for the government. The saved investment could be reallocated to other crucial social sector, for instance funding primary art education or supporting more galleries. Such policy could establish a tolerant and responsible image for the authorities, wining public support at the same time.

Last but not least, more diversified options for art appreciation can be provided to the audience. A more liberal society encourages more in-depth communication among artists and audience, which lays a solid foundation for the mutual fusion and absorption from various forms of art. More innovations, resulting from the cultural exchange, would arise, providing more abundant possibilities for art appreciation among the citizens. The options for leisure time entertainment together with the local cultural diversity would eventually be enriched.

By way of conclusion, after logical thinking all sorts of freedom of the artists, I strongly support the view that the liberty of the artists should be guaranteed under any circumstances.

Thinking of law

Law is not an eternal rule, but a choice of human. In the same way，law is not the final answer given by the God. Law is the choice of people. It often contains the judgment, measurement and comparison of different values in our society, and it contains many important choices made by our society as well.

Let’s take some very ancient cases as examples. As we all know, the United States is an immigrant society. At the beginning, there were numerous people who came to the United States by vessel like the primeval one called Mayflower. Apart from the no religious wars, one of the most essential attractions is the vast and uninhabited land in America. Therefore, the judicial system of the United States was first established. It was all about property rights law, that is, how to protect everyone’s private property. Later, a series of other laws, such as contract law and criminal law, were developed on the basis of property rights law.

So until today, when we get into a law school to study, one of the first year’s compulsory courses is property law. But I really didn’t expect that we lived in such a complex business society with such a complex property right relationship.

law in the cinema

During this Spring festival,a great number of films are on show. Whilst, I notice a unsightly behavior which makes some people uncomfortable——using phones to take a picture of the film screen.
I think this demeanor at least is a kind of infringement. It first violates the right of other audiences to watch the film without interference. Why should we be disturbed by this unethical behavior when we all spend the same money to have fun watching movies.
At the same time,it infringes the copyright of the movie. I think this kind of screen photography is usually published on social media, otherwise it will be meaningless for people who take pictures on the screen. It means that he is using an abnormal way to reproduce part of the content of a work, and its quality is definitely greatly reduced. For creators or publishers, their works are distorted to a certain extent. This is a loss to the original author. In view of the fact that copyright should be a right initiated only by the infringed party, screen photography may be the lightest of many infringements (screen photography is not really different from stealing photography), and copyright owners may not be investigated, but those who do such acts should not legitimize screen photography in the name of “sharing” or “helping propaganda”.
I also searched the Internet,in Hong Kong,there is a fine of about 50000 RMB and imprisonment for 3 months. I am looking forward that Chinese government will make stricter laws like this in the future.

Labor Market

Yesterday in the economic lessons,I leaned about the knowledge of “Labor Market” which is closely related to our real life.

There are many people finding works,but the occupations are scarce. As a result,there are also a wide range of factors that may influence a person’s choice of occupations including wage factors,non-wage factors and other limiting factors.

Wage factors,of course,is the most important element. Generally speaking,the higher the salaries on offer,the more a person would want to do the job. Wage rate means the basic wage a worker receives per unit. I think if I need to find a job,I may think of it first. Due to the life quantity depends on incomes a lot. A high bonuses or commission may attract someone too,but not every job has extra bonuses.

Moreover,some other non-wage factors may be attached importance to by people. I will list a few the most principal reasons. First,some jobs can provide a high degree of job satisfaction,which would result in a higher degree of motivation. For example,most teachers have happiness during their whole teaching career. Not only the success of their students in study,but also receiving thanks from parents. That is the pride of all their lifetime. Second,the length of holidays makes a big difference. Although the government have made laws to set down a minimum length for full-time workers. In Western countries,some companies may provide more holidays for delivery mothers,newly married couples and so on.

From this lesson,I learned that everyone have their different choices for a job. All in all,the most suitable one is the best!
