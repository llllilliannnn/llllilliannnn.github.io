mock trail competition

This week，I put in a great deal of effort on preparing for the Mock Trial Competition and finished the preliminary contest successfully with my friends .

There are four parts which are Opening Statements, Direct Examination, Cross Examination and Closing Statements.

I was the lawyer,so my most important mission is to protect my witness and prove our responsibility is smaller than the other side.

At first,I read large amount of materials to know about every character .I helped my witness to think of the both questions and answers to write manuscript of direct examination .At the same time,I must write the questions to ask the opposite .It is really hard experiences.I was asked to rewrite again and again by the senior older schoolmate.

Then,I had a short talk with the lawyers in the con side .We introduced some information about our own witness to each other .It can save lots of time .But in the real competition,this behavior would not be allowed.

Finally,I also wrote the closing argument which is a big work. In the last days,I should memorize my words as more as possible.

In general ,participating in mock trial competition is not only good to the cultivation of students’ legal consciousness, social awareness, civic awareness and team cooperation awareness, but also helps to improve our players’ self-confidence, logical analysis ability, language organization ability and on-the-spot response ability.

2020-11-28

summary of writing skills

In this whole week,I had lessons on how to do a summary writing excellently in ESL exams. Here are several tips I have learned.

First,we should pay attention to the instruction information to find out the purpose. Then,highlight the keywords in the text. There are usually over 8 points in all. In addition,organizing them well is important and we do not have to use them in order. Next,the most important tip is not only trying to use our own words with same meanings as much as possible,but also using linking verbs and phrases for example thank to(to explain why something happened),despite this(to contrast ideas),before this (to say when something happened) and so on. Finally,our summary must be within the word limit which is around 100 or 120 words.

Although this learning process is a little boring,I truly got this skill in the end. Just as the saying goes:”Nothing is difficult to the man who will try”.All in all,come on！

2020-11-21

my volunteer test

Last weekend,I participated in the volunteer assessment of Xiao Ling of Ming Density which is the only world cultural heritage in Nanjing .It was such a precious experience.

The owner of the tomb was Zhu yuanzhang who was the first emperor and the founder of the Ming Dynasty .In the museum,there was a large mural of the whole view,plenty of works of art .They are all the symbol of history .So I could improve my knowledge by looking around. Inside the park,there were a few deer .I could not stop taking a close look at them.

During this exam,I met lots of excellent contemporaries .Although I have passed it successfully,I learned a lot from them .First,pay attention to every detail ,just like zipping up the coat well when guiding visitors .Second, try to be flexible when meeting problems instead of remaining silence .Third,take to things no matter you do first .This is so-called interest is the best teacher.

The interview was over .I am now looking forward to be the volunteer work next month .Come on!
