arranging blackboard newspaper

As a member of General Technology Publicity Department in the Student Union，this week，we arranged the blackboard newspaper in our activity classroom.

In the first period，we collected a large amount of posters from three grades. These posters were about different subjects like Economics,Chemistry and so on. Then,we classified the posters by colors. In fact ,some tabloids were just monochrome and concise. If we put this kind all together, the background might look ugly. After a short discussion with other members and presidents of the union,we decided to put less colorful posters in the corners in order,then decorating the main parts with magnificent posters instead.

By one-hour hard work,all the posters were put up on the blackboard. However, there were still many open spaces in the blackboard. So we planned to decorate it with some colorful papers,stickers and cute pictures. Moreover，I went to teacher’s office to lend two boxes of drawing pins to fix these ornaments. The best part was that we drew some pictures with crayons by ourselves，for instance，red loving hearts，small cats，crash. We all had a good time in the process.

Every time I saw the beautiful blackboard newspaper , I felt proud of myself very much. From this experience，I learned the importance of the cooperation and we should balance our different ideas to achieve the best result. Hence,we may be more efficient.
