my successful group presentation

This week,my group finished the most excellent presentation by joint efforts in ESL lessons. There are other five group members which are Judy, Amy, Jar, Victory and Bill. In such a short time,we should do a survey about transportation ,process and analyze data ,draw graphs ,make a ppt and so on.

At weekends,we did a survey both online and offline. About 69 students and teachers finished our surveys with 20 questions. These questions were carefully designed by us,including :How old are you?How long does it take to take to get to school?

​ What kind of transport do you usually use?

​ What do you think of this transport?

​ What problems do you meet when going outside?

​ Do you like cars used electricity or petrol?

​ ……

Then we started to make a large number of histograms and pie charts. My main job was the most difficult. I needed to draw conclusions in the end. By comparing different figures and summarizing the similar facts,I found that factors like time,safety,age all influenced each other.

Also, I learned many general presentation language. At first,we should give a greeting ,welcome and introduce ourselves. Then,we need to give the topic and aims. In addition,content is necessary too. Between sections,handing over to a teammate was needed to men. Finally,as the reporters,we were willing to answer anyone’s questions.

I was proud of every member of my team. What a pleasant cooperation!
