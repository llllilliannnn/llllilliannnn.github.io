My self-introduction

I am Lillian who is a 15-year-old girl from Nanjing. Many people think that I am very gentle, careful and imaginative . At home ,with the status of a daughter,a granddaughter and a sister,I am very helpful and attentive to my siblings and parents,for example ,I wash dishes twice a week after dinner.
My friends consider me as a girl who enjoys life to the full, I like dancing, singing and watching art exhibitions in my free time. As a result, we usually participate in art festivals together. Last week ，we danced Forever Young together in the stage during the Christmas Gala. Also, there seems to be a consensus that I enjoy trying new things which make me come out of my own comfort zone. For instance , this term, I participated in the Students’ Union and was successful to be a member in the union.
In most team work activities, my classmates choose me to be the leader from time to time,due to the fact that I am not only be a risk taker with excellent leadership and communication skills,but also a coordinator who can bring harmony to groups and delegate well and fairly.
So far，one of my proudest achievements is persisting in playing the piano for more than eight years. When I started learning that, I was in kindergarten. There are several reasons why I take pride in it. Firstly, just as the saying goes “Practice makes perfect.”. I spend over an hour playing it every day and it has become part of my daily routine. Secondly, I have become a music fan during this process. I particularly enjoy R&B music，classical style and pop music.
At weekends，I go to live house as soon as I am free because it can make me relax after a busy day. In addition, I am learning the Ukulele to satisfy my deep musical interest. In general,The piano has become like a best friend to me and will forever be part of me and music has taught me so much such as being patient, focused and creative. It also allows me to lose myself in a different world.
