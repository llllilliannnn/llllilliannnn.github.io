Let artists possess freedom !

The restrictions imposed by the government are growing increasingly strict according to public opinion. I therefore argue that all the unnecessary regulations in art industry should be abolished.

First and foremost, freedom serves as the most indispensable factor in facilitating the creation of artists. It offers more sufficient space for such creator, stimulating more inspiration and imagination during the creation and thus enabling them to thoroughly convey their inner critics about social reality without any concerns. As a result, more unique and impressive art works would arise, which contributes to the fame and fortune of the authors. Apparently, without adequate liberty, the artists could hardly achieve their career success.

On top of this, government can also benefit significantly from allowing the artists to create without limitation. Via abolishing those unnecessary regulations in art industry, a considerable amount of administrative expense can be reduced effectively, saving the finite fiscal budgets for the government. The saved investment could be reallocated to other crucial social sector, for instance funding primary art education or supporting more galleries. Such policy could establish a tolerant and responsible image for the authorities, wining public support at the same time.

Last but not least, more diversified options for art appreciation can be provided to the audience. A more liberal society encourages more in-depth communication among artists and audience, which lays a solid foundation for the mutual fusion and absorption from various forms of art. More innovations, resulting from the cultural exchange, would arise, providing more abundant possibilities for art appreciation among the citizens. The options for leisure time entertainment together with the local cultural diversity would eventually be enriched.

By way of conclusion, after logical thinking all sorts of freedom of the artists, I strongly support the view that the liberty of the artists should be guaranteed under any circumstances.
